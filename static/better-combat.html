Better Combat 1.16.5 Port

Better Combat adds an animated combat system based on Minecraft dungeons.
Instead of struggling to hover over people with your mouse, Better Combat tracks your weapons collisions with monsters throughout its animation to apply damage. You can even see your weapon animations in first person! Datapacks can add animation definitions for weapons from any mod and many are already supported by automatic fallback compatibility. Custom weapon animations are created in blender. Learn More
