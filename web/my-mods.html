Luke's Mods

I'm taking mod commissions for Forge or Fabric versions 1.18.x, 1.17.x or 1.16.x.
DM me on discord (LukeGrahamLandry#6888) or read the commissions page for more info.

Mimic

The stealthy mimic mob looks exactly like a chest but attacks when opened! They spawn in place of loot chests, kill them to claim your treasure. They can also be tamed and used as portable storage. Forge 1.16.5 & 1.18.2

Mountables

The spiritual successor to Animal Bikes. Craft 24 mountable versions of your favorite vanilla creatures. They can be upgraded with speed, flight, fire proof and more. Forge 1.16.5

Mercenaries

The world is full of scary monsters but never fear, you can build an army to defend yourself! Hire mercenaries and equip them with any weapons. Forge 1.16.5

Tribes

Inspired by the Factions plugin. Allows players to form groups. Claim land, manage ranks, kick/ban players, choose a deity and level up your tribe to gain always active potion effects. Most features are configurable. Forge 1.16.5 and 1.17.1

Inclusive Enchanting

Allows most enchantments to apply to extra items. For example punch/flame crossbows, quick charge bows, and fire aspect tools (acts as auto-smelt). Forge 1.16.5

Harder Core

Adds a thirst, temperature and air quality bars that punish being in hostile environments. Deadly shadows when you enter darkness and blood moons. When you die, a grave guarded by your corpse holds your items. Forge 1.16.5

Find My Friends

Renders player name plates over any distance so you always know how to find your way back, even when they're outside your render distance. Forge 1.16.5, 1.17.1, 1.18.1
