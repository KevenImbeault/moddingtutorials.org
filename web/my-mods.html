Luke's Mods

I work with Forge and Fabric versions 1.17.x, 1.16.x and 1.15.x

$VIDEOS
Mimic

The stealthy mimic mob looks exactly like a chest but attacks when opened! They spawn in place of loot chests, kill them to claim your treasure. They can also be tamed and used as portable storage. Available for Forge 1.16.5

Mountables

The spiritual successor to Animal Bikes. Craft mountable versions of your favourite vanilla creatures. They can be upgraded with speed, flight, fire proof and more. Available for Forge 1.16.5

Tribes

Inspired by the Factions plugin. Allows players to form groups. Claim land, manage ranks, kick/ban players, choose a deity and level up your tribe to gain always active potion effects. Most features are configurable. Available for Forge 1.16.5 and Forge 1.17.1

Inclusive Enchanting

Allows most enchantments to apply to extra items. For example punch/flame crossbows, quick charge bows, and fire aspect tools (acts as auto-smelt). Available for Forge 1.16.5

Harder Core

Adds a thirst bar that must be managed like hunger. Temperature and air quality that punish being in hostile environments. Deadly shadows when you enter darkness and blood moons (1/8 of nights have much higher mob spawns). To help you out, a grave holds your items when you die so they don't despawn (but its guarded by your corpse). Available for Forge 1.16.5
